Tips to Conserve Electricity

1. Turn Off Lights When Not in Use

Make it a habit to switch off lights when leaving a room to save electricity.

2. Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

Invest in appliances with the Energy Star label to reduce energy consumption.

3. Unplug Electronics When Not in Use

Electronics and chargers continue to use energy even when not in use. Unplug them or use power strips to easily turn off multiple devices.

4. Adjust Thermostat Settings

Set your thermostat a few degrees lower in winter and higher in summer to save on heating and cooling costs.

5. Use Natural Light and Ventilation

Open curtains and windows during the day to utilize natural light and reduce the need for artificial lighting and air conditioning.
