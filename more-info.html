Why Should We Choose DLLE

Why You Should Join DLLE as a Student

1. Personal and Professional Growth

  • Enhance leadership, communication, and project management skills.
  • Boost academic performance with additional marks.
  • Gain practical experience for better career readiness.

2. Community Engagement and Social Responsibility

  • Make a tangible difference in societal issues.
  • Develop empathy and social responsibility.

3. Networking and Collaboration

  • Collaborate with diverse peers.
  • Receive mentorship from experienced professionals.

4. Holistic Education Experience

  • Combine classroom learning with practical experiences.
  • Embrace lifelong learning through continuous skill acquisition.

5. Recognition and Rewards

  • Earn additional marks for active participation.
  • Gain recognition through awards and competitions like UDAAN.

6. Early Involvement and Long-Term Benefits

  • Build a strong foundation for future involvement.
  • Enhance your resume with valuable experiences.
Vocational and Community Projects

Vocational Career Oriented Projects

  • Career Project (CP): Guidance on alternative career options.
  • Industry Orientation Project (IOP): Exposure to industry environments.
  • Anna Poorna Yojana (APY): Promoting entrepreneurship through food-related businesses.
  • Skill Development (SD): Enhancing employability through skill training.

Community Oriented Projects

  • Population Education Club (PEC): Raising awareness on population issues.
  • Survey Research: Conducting surveys to gather data on various social issues.
  • Education for All (EFA): Promoting education through open and distance learning.
  • Environment Education: Raising awareness on environmental issues.
  • Civic Sense (CS): Promoting civic responsibilities among students.
  • Consumer Guidance: Educating students about consumer rights and responsibilities.
